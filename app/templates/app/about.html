Rice Bikes is an app designed to facilitate the internal processes of the Rice Bike Shop.




The development team.

Rice Bikes is a project of Rice Apps, a student-led organization dedicated to making students' lives better through technology. We're completely open-source, and built on Python (Django), SQLite, Bootstrap CSS, and a ton of fantastic JS libraries including jQuery, AngularJS, and PaceJS.
